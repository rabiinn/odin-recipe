Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish that features layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's hearty, comforting, and perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet with the ground beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
  10. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
