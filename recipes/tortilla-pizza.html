Tortilla Pizza

Description

Tortilla pizza is a quick and easy alternative to traditional pizza. Using tortillas as the base, you can customize it with your favorite toppings for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Spices

Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. Spread tomato sauce on the tortilla.

3. Add cheese and your choice of vegetables.

4. Sprinkle spices on top.

5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted.
